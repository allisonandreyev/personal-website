allisonmandreyev [at] gmail [dot] com

I'm a high school student interested in robotics and AI, with a focus on vision-language agents and neurosymbolic AI for robotics applications. A field I haven't gotten much experience in but would like to explore are world models and AI-driven 3D/4D environment construction.

In my most recent projects I have collaborated with [Tian Lan](http://www2.seas.gwu.edu/~tlan/) at George Washington University on AR safety and computer vision, [Xuesu Xiao](https://people.cs.gmu.edu/~xiao/) at George Mason University's [RobotiXX Lab](https://robotixx.cs.gmu.edu/) on robotics simulation, [Mark Klein](https://cci.mit.edu/klein-old/bio/) at MIT on LLMs for deliberation, and previously at UVA and the [Maryland Robotics Center](https://robotics.umd.edu/) at UMD. 

I've also been lucky to participate in the [UMD CPSE Program](https://cpse.umd.edu/) in which I developed SignScribe, and later interned under to develop [GRASP](/projects/grasp). I am a part of the [Rockville Science Center](https://www.rockvillesciencecenter.org/), where I have helped coordinate and run [Rockville Science Day](https://www.rockvillesciencecenter.org/rockville-science-day) for the past few years.

Outside of research I am a DJ and a public forum debater. I gave a [TEDx talk](https://youtu.be/KtK2N6RDOrY?si=o6vRYcolPZ7EjQwH) on citizen science and AI in research, and I write about my projects on my blog and Medium. If you'd like to get in contact, check out my [GitHub](https://github.com/allisonandreyev), [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/allisonandreyev/), or social links in the website footer.
